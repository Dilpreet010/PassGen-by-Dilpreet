How to Use PassGen

Set Password Length: Use the slider to choose the desired length of your password.
Select Options: Choose to include numbers, symbols, uppercase, and lowercase letters.
Generate Password: Click on the "Generate Password" button.
Copy Password: Click on the copy icon to copy the generated password to your clipboard.
Reset: Click the Reset button to clear all selections and reset the password length to its default value.
